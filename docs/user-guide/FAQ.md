# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

### Is Ancore Wallet custodial?

No. You control your wallet credentials and recovery phrase.

### Which network should I use?

Use Testnet for experimentation and Mainnet for production funds.

## Accounts and Backups

### What happens if I forget my password?

Use your recovery phrase to restore wallet access, then set a new password.

### Can support recover my wallet for me?

No. Support cannot recover your wallet without your recovery phrase.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Yes. You can maintain multiple wallet accounts for different purposes.

## Transactions

### Why is my payment pending?

Possible causes:

- Temporary network congestion
- Slow confirmation finality
- Sender/recipient network mismatch

### Why did my transaction fail?

Typical reasons:

- Insufficient balance (including fees)
- Invalid recipient address
- Connectivity interruptions

### Can I reverse a sent payment?

No. Confirm all details before submitting.

## Session Keys

### Are session keys safe?

Yes, when created with limited permissions and short lifetimes.

### When should I revoke a session key?

Revoke immediately when:

- A workflow is complete
- A key is no longer needed
- You suspect unauthorized activity

## Security

### Should I store my recovery phrase digitally?

Prefer offline storage. If digital storage is required, use encrypted, audited tooling.

### How often should I rotate credentials?

Review security settings monthly and rotate credentials after any suspected compromise.
